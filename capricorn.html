Capricorn: The Tenth Sign of the Zodiac

Capricorn, the tenth sign in the zodiac cycle, spans from December 22 to January 19. Represented by the symbol of the Goat, this earth sign is associated with traits such as determination, ambition, and practicality. As we delve into the intricacies of Capricorn, we uncover the profound ways in which its celestial attributes shape its natives' personalities and life paths.
